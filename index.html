Signature Case Study — PM Path

PM Path is an execution framework applied to a venture-style concept: an affordable hybrid safety solution combining a low-cost wearable panic device with a smartphone app powered by emotional AI and facial recognition.
The goal is not just the product itself, but demonstrating how structured execution systems can be designed across ambiguity, risk, and scaling constraints.

PM Path is not primarily a product exercise. It is a leadership exercise in structuring ambiguity, aligning stakeholders, and reducing execution risk across technical feasibility, business viability, user trust, regulatory constraints, and scalability readiness.
